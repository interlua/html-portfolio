LUA's favorite movies

Some of which she has indeed watched!

HELLRAISER

This movie is such a silly scary movie. I love it because it's not all that scary but quite gorey. I loved the storyline, so much scandal and irony. I wish the monsters had more screentime but they did in the second movie.

HOUSE (1970)

This movie was ahead of its time!!! The script, the acting, the costumes, the effects! It's a playful movie and visually pleasing to watch.

PARASITE

When this movie first came out and everyone was hyping it up, I didn't care to watch it. But when I did a few years after, I realized it deserved all the attention it got. This movie is a cinematic masterpiece. It's funny, it keeps you on your toes, and there are so many motifs that you see throughout the film that touch on a lot of the problems within the world today.
